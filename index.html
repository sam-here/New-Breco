About Us

Incubus India was officially founded by S Ramesh Kumar in 2011. It was born out of a passion for portable electronics and accessories. We started our journey as electronics and gadgets distributers for select brands and products with a greater vision to introduce the best global brands to Indian markets at reasonable prices.

We partner with renowned consumer electronics companies from around the world. Within a brief period of time, we have established reliable partnerships with global brands and local retail partners.

Today, we are one of the top distributors for brands like Sony and Jabra in India. Incubus India creates a unique experience for electronics and gadget aficionados. With over 700 products in our portfolio, we match every customer with the right and quality accessories.
